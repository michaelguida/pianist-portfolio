## Campaign setup — quick reference

- **Page it runs from:** Dr. Michael Guida's Piano Studio (Facebook)
- **Pixel:** installed on the-serious-student page, dormant until `META_PIXEL_ID` is set;
  fires a `Lead` event on application submit.
- **Objective:** Leads, conversion location = Website, optimize for the `Lead` event.
  (If a brand-new pixel gets too little data, fall back to Traffic / Landing Page Views to start.)
- **Audience:** Scottsdale, Paradise Valley, Arcadia, North Scottsdale + radius. Age ~30–60.
  Broad works for local high-ticket; can layer interests (classical music, piano) if needed.
- **Budget:** start ~$15–20/day.
- **Schedule:** auto-start ~Aug 1 (a couple days before returning home Aug 2–3), so warm
  applications are waiting instead of a cold start, and no lead-fielding during vacation.
- **Creative:** launch with the masterclass photo (4:5 or 1:1). Swap in the 60-sec video later as a test.

## Rules (house style)
- No sentence-splitting em dashes.
- No income callouts ("if you make over $X") — violates Meta's personal-attributes policy.
- Premium implied through identity, not price shaming. "Not cheap / few spots / by application" does the filtering.
