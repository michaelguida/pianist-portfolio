About Dr. Michael Guida

A lifelong dedication to the art of piano and the joy of teaching.

Scottsdale - McCormick Ranch, AZ
michaeltguida@gmail.com

Dr. Michael Guida is an award-winning pianist and dedicated educator with over two decades of experience in classical music, bringing world-class training to students of all ages and levels.

Raised in Arizona, Dr. Guida began playing piano at age 7 and has cultivated his passion for performing and teaching ever since. His musical journey took him from the Southwest to some of the nation's finest music programs.

He earned both his Bachelor and Master of Music degrees in Piano Performance from the University of Denver, where he studied under the renowned pianist Steven Mayer. He continued his training at the prestigious University of Texas at Austin, where he was awarded a full tuition scholarship to complete his Doctor of Musical Arts degree under the guidance of the internationally acclaimed pianist Anton Nel.

During his graduate studies, Dr. Guida also received advanced pedagogical training from Martha Hilley, who served as President of the Music Teachers National Association. This comprehensive training equipped him with both exceptional performance skills and the knowledge to teach at the highest level.

Performance & Specialization

Dr. Guida has performed extensively across the United States, captivating audiences with his musical sensitivity and technical command. He has developed particular expertise in Edvard Grieg's Lyric Pieces, the focus of his doctoral dissertation, which examined their folk elements and character while exploring how they can be used as a teaching tool for students at every level.

Recent Arizona performances include appearances at the Scottsdale Automotive Museum, Piano Gallery, and the Bösendorfer Salon Series at Riverton Piano, where he continues to share his artistry with the local community.

Teaching Philosophy

At the University of Texas at Austin, Dr. Guida taught both group piano classes and maintained a private studio of students, refining his pedagogical approach and developing innovative teaching methods that make complex concepts accessible.

Now based in Scottsdale - McCormick Ranch area, he maintains his own private studio where he works with students from beginner through advanced collegiate level. His teaching combines rigorous technical training with a patient, encouraging approach that brings out the best in every student. In 2024, he was named Top Steinway Teacher at Steinway & Sons Scottsdale, a recognition of that dedication to his students.

Dr. Guida remains actively engaged with the broader musical community, providing his students with opportunities to participate in festivals, competitions, and ensemble experiences.
