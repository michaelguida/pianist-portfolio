Dr. Michael Guida

Recipient of the Steinway & Sons Scottsdale Top Teacher Award in both 2024 and 2025, Dr. Guida brings world-class training, a Doctorate of Musical Arts, and over two decades of experience to his in-home studio in Scottsdale.

What makes his teaching special? He makes everything easy to learn. His passion for music is infectious, and he has a gift for helping people master the art of playing the piano.
