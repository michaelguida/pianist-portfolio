Common Questions

Do I need a piano at home?

Yes, and if you currently do not own one, I can help find you a piano that suits your budget and space.

How long are lessons?

I offer 30, 45, and 60 minute lessons depending on the needs of the student.

What ages do you teach?

All ages. It's never too early or too late to start!

Where are lessons held?

Lessons are held in my professional in-home studio in Scottsdale - McCormick Ranch area.

What if I've never played before?

Wonderful! Complete beginners are always welcome. I'll guide you from your very first note.

Do you teach online/virtual lessons?

Yes! While I prefer in-person lessons at my studio in Scottsdale, I also offer high-quality virtual lessons for students who need that flexibility.

How often are lessons scheduled?

Lessons are scheduled weekly to ensure consistent progress and skill development.

How much should I practice between lessons?

Consistency over time spent always wins in the long run. However, the more the better - 30 minutes or more daily is recommended for steady progress.

Do you prepare students for competitions or exams?

Yes! I tailor my teaching to each student's needs - whether you're playing just for fun and enjoyment, or preparing for competitions and recitals.
