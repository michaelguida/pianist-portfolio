The Beginning of Your Journey

Wondering what learning actually looks like? Here's what the first few months typically look like for most students.

1

Your First Lesson

Get comfortable at the keyboard, find your way around the keys, and play your very first notes. No experience required.

2

Your First Song

By the end of your very first lesson, you'll already be playing a simple song with both hands. New pieces follow faster than you'd expect.

3

Reading the Music

Learn to read notes and rhythms at a natural pace, so you can start picking up new pieces on your own.

4

Playing for Others

Build the confidence to share a piece with family and friends whenever you feel ready, and never before.
