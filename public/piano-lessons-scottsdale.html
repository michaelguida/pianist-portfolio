2

3

Learn with Independence

Develop the reading and practice skills to begin exploring new music on your own.

4

Make the Music Your Own

Add expression, imagination, and personality as your playing develops.

Lessons in Scottsdale - McCormick Ranch

Lessons take place at Dr. Guida's private studio in Scottsdale's McCormick Ranch area, where students learn on a carefully maintained Steinway grand piano in a quiet setting devoted to music. Weekly lessons last 30, 45, or 60 minutes, depending on each student's age, level, and goals.

Students may also participate in recitals, festivals, competitions, and ensemble opportunities throughout the year.
